A Review

Since March 2015, we've rocked 20+ pub gigs, 10 festivals including Ipswich's Music in the Park Day and 2 live radio sessions. We also supported blues legends, Nine Below Zero in November.

For 2016, we're looking to score some London gigs and finish our first album. We've been asked to support another major blues band too, but we're not quite ready to share the details with you just yet...

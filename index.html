News

Supporting Nine Below Zero

At Venue 77

We're excited to announce that we'll be supporting the legendary blues band, Nine Below Zero on 21st November! Tickets are £15 in advance from The Gardeners Arms, Ipswich Tourist Information Centre and Out of Time Records or £20 on the door. We can't wait, see you there!

Supporting Mouthful of Ashtrays

At The Bull, Colchester

This will be a great show! Mouthful of Ashtrays are a great band and we will be supporting them at The Bull in Colchester on October 24th. Make sure you don't miss out!

Live on ICR FM

From the Music Room

We're excited to announce that we'll be playing live on Ipswich Community Radio from The Music Room on 25 August from 7.30pm. Sharing the good times are our friends from Essex, the mighty Mouthful of Ashtrays! It's gonna be a blast, see you there!

Ipswich Music Day

Ed Sheeran Stage

We're delighted to announce that we'll be playing Ipswich Music Day at Christchurch Park, Ipswich on 5 July 2015! We're playing at 5:30pm on the Suffolk New College / Ed Sheeran stage. We've known about this one for a while and had to keep it under wraps but the full lineup has just been announced, going to be a great day of music, love and good times so we hope to see you all there!

BBC Radio Suffolk

Stephen Foster's Drive Time

Tune in to Drive Time between 6 and 7pm this week (or listen again) to hear one of our songs recorded at the station earlier this year. Big thanks to Foz for the airtime - much appreciated!

New dates added!

Iptopia, Grinning Rat and Bullstock

Iptopia is a charity evening for the British Heart Foundation and Diabetes UK. Also playing are The Handsome Dead, Joe Hunt and Finding Frank. At the Grinning Rat we'll be supported by our friends Busking for Breakfast. At this year's Bullstock we'll be playing in The Soundhouse on the same stage as fellow Ipswichian, Benjamin Bloom.

Battle of the Bands

Ipswich May Day Festival

Thank You to everyone who came out and supported us at The Steamboat last night! We appreciate it more than you know. You being there means that we won the spot to play at the Ipswich May Day Festival on Sunday 3rd May 2015! We would also like to thank Tony Shevlin for inviting us and the other talented bands that took part!

Felixstowe Radio

The Band Show

We'll be playing live and talking about our music and more on 16 May 2015 from 6pm. Tune in via the Interwebs or 107.5FM.

We need your support!

Battle of the Bands

On 27th March we will be playing a 30 minute set at The Steamboat Tavern as part of The May Day Festival fund raiser. There will be 3 bands playing and the amount of people we bring will enhance our chances of playing at the festival itself! Everyone gets a free CD! It's also a great chance to get a little taster of what to expect at The Cock and Pye on 17th April!

Severn FM

The Blues Hour

Dr. Wart Hoover played our debut EP track "Nineteen Nine One" on his excellent radio show, The Blues Hour. It's a great show - tune in over the web at Severn FM.

BBC Suffolk Introducing...

Live Room

We were live in session on BBC Radio Suffolk's BBC Suffolk Introducing... show on the 9 January 2015! We recorded a few tunes for them in their live room and a had a chat about the band's beginnings among other things. We had great fun doing it so hope you guys enjoyed listening in.

Festival Season

Summer is here!

Well, almost. Pack your sun cream / brolly and picnic and catch us at Cosmic Puffin 9 on Mersea Island, Ipswich May Day Festival, StowBlues at the Museum of East Anglian Life, Colchester's Big Sunday - we'll be playing at the Three Wise Monkeys, Music on the Green in Stowupland, Ipswich Music Day, Bures Music Festival, Kelvedon Community Festival and Sax Music Fest. Check out the gig dates below.

Supporting Band of Friends

at the John Peel Centre

We're excited to announce that we'll be supporting Band of Friends - band mates of the late, great Rory Gallagher!

Put May 21st in your calendar and grab your tickets from the John Peel Centre - it's gonna be one helluva show!

And that was 2015

A Review

Since March 2015, we've rocked 20+ pub gigs, 10 festivals including Ipswich's Music in the Park Day and 2 live radio sessions. We also supported blues legends, Nine Below Zero in November.

For 2016, we're looking to score some London gigs and finish our first album. We've been asked to support another major blues band too, but we're not quite ready to share the details with you just yet...
